Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how FoodCheck ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

Images you provide. When you use the App to scan a food label, you capture or select an image using your device's camera or photo gallery. This image is sent to a third-party AI service for analysis and is not stored on our servers.

Scan history. The App stores your scan results (product names, nutritional data, glycemic scores, thumbnail images, and timestamps) locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to export or share it.

App settings. Your preferences (selected AI model, language) are stored locally on your device.

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Account or login information (the App does not require an account)

2. How We Use Information

The information processed by the App is used solely to:

  • Analyze food label images and extract nutritional information
  • Calculate glycemic index and glycemic load scores
  • Provide health-related recommendations based on nutritional data
  • Maintain your local scan history for your reference

3. Third-Party AI Services

The App sends food label images to third-party AI services for analysis. Depending on your settings, this may include:

  • Google Gemini (Gemini Flash) — subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Claude (Claude Opus) — subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy

Images sent to these services are used solely for the purpose of analyzing the food label content. We recommend reviewing the respective privacy policies of these providers for details on how they handle data.

4. Data Storage and Security

All scan history, settings, and app data are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your personal data. Since data is stored on-device, its security depends on your device's own security measures (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share your data with third parties, except as described in Section 3 (third-party AI services used for food label analysis).

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your scan history is stored on your device for as long as you choose to keep it. You can delete individual scan entries or your entire scan history at any time from within the App. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect personal information from any user, no special provisions are required.

8. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of capturing food label images for analysis. Camera access is used only when you actively initiate a scan. The App does not access your camera in the background.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version of this page with a revised "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
