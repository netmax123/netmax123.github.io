Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

AllergyScore ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Contact: developer4565@gmail.com

1. Information We Collect

Personal Health Information

  • Symptom logs: Severity ratings, symptoms, and triggers you record
  • Allergy profile: Allergens and pets you configure during onboarding
  • Medications: Medications you choose to log
  • Sleep data: Sleep duration imported from Android Health Connect (with your permission)

Location Data

  • Device location: Latitude and longitude used to fetch local weather, air quality, and pollen data (with your permission)
  • City searches: Cities you search for on the Relocation screen

Usage Analytics

We use Amplitude to collect anonymous usage analytics such as screen views, feature interactions, and app lifecycle events. These analytics help us improve the app and do not include your health data.

Purchase Information

Subscription status is managed through Google Play Billing. We do not directly collect or store your payment information — this is handled entirely by Google.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide core functionality: Your symptom logs, allergy profile, and location data are used to generate allergy scores, pollen forecasts, and personalized insights.
  • AI-powered insights: Anonymized health summaries (symptom severity, triggers, sleep, pollen levels, air quality) are sent to our secure backend server to generate weekly reports and daily briefs. No personally identifiable information (name, email, or precise address) is included in these requests.
  • Weather and pollen data: Your location is sent to Open-Meteo (a third-party weather API) to retrieve local weather, air quality, and pollen forecasts.
  • Maps: Your location is used with Google Maps to display pollen information on a map.
  • Improve the app: Anonymous usage analytics help us understand which features are most valuable and identify issues.

3. Data Storage

  • All personal health data (symptom logs, allergy profiles, sleep records) is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database.
  • We do not store your health data on our servers after processing AI insights.
  • Preferences and settings are stored locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

5. Permissions

  • Location (Fine & Coarse): To fetch local pollen, weather, and air quality data
  • Internet: To communicate with weather APIs and our insight backend
  • Health Connect (Sleep): To import sleep duration for correlation with allergy symptoms
  • Notifications: To send daily allergy briefs (optional)
  • Bluetooth: Used by Health Connect integration on older Android versions

All permissions are optional and requested at the point of use. The app functions with reduced features if permissions are denied.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We share data only as described in Section 4 (third-party services) and only to the extent necessary to provide the app's features.

7. Children's Privacy

AllergyScore is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at developer4565@gmail.com.

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All health data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data.
  • Anonymous analytics data retained by Amplitude is subject to Amplitude's data retention policies.
  • You can clear all app data at any time through your device's Settings > Apps > AllergyScore > Clear Data.

9. Your Choices

  • Location: You can revoke location permission at any time in device settings.
  • Health Connect: You can disconnect sleep data access through Health Connect settings.
  • Notifications: You can disable notifications in device settings.
  • Delete data: Uninstall the app or clear app data to remove all locally stored information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
